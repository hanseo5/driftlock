# Driftlock Office Hours

Turn the user's raw request into a grounded product conversation.

## Workflow

1. Identify target user, job, desired outcome, constraints, non-goals, risks, and examples.
2. Ask only product-impacting questions that cannot be inferred from evidence.
3. Record unresolved ambiguity for brainstorm or grill.
4. Output office-hours.md and route to driftlock-brainstorm.

## Rules

- Do not write implementation tasks.
- Do not lock product intent.
- Do not ask the user to decide mechanical implementation details.

## Output

Produce `office-hours.md`. Read `references/office-hours-contract.md` for the exact contract.
